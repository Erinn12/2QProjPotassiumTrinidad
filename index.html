Students' Self-Care and Welfare

What is Self-Care and Why Should it be Practiced?

Self-care is considered as anything you do to take care of yourself to stay physically, mentally, and emotionally well. During the pandemic, students would often forget to take care of themselves because of the bottomless pile of schoolwork. This website will discuss easy self-care tips for busy students at home to accomplish their daily activities well.


Taking care of oneself is essential to overcome anxiety, trauma, and burnout. A good self-care routine improves concentration and increases happiness and improves energy. Self-care can help you identify your responses to stress and develop coping skills to manage them. Self-care encourages you to maintain a healthy relationship with yourself to transmit good feelings to others.
